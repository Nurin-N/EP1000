EP1000 Digital Fabrication and Prototyping

Digital Fabrication and Prototyping (DFAB), is a module that focuses on teching Digital Fabrication methods for designing and creating prototypes.In the process, one would get to learn and experience how to document/ publish their work online as well as skills for computer guided techniques, 3D printing and laser cutting to create a prototype.

This site was created as a part of the process in this module where students would be able to learn and document their progress of work while also learn design fundamentals and programming/ coding techniques to create a website.
