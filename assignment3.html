Version control

We were tasked to learn how to extract back any data/information lost through git. This can be easily applied if one were to accidentaly delete a portion of text or even would like to undo a mistake but have already pushed it to the repository.

  • Go to the GitHub Desktop and click under history
  • Right click on the section you would like to commit and revert
  • Push it through and gain the lost data back
